User question: I need to check how long it takes for my shipment to arrive

We understand your eagerness to know the estimated time it will take for your shipment to arrive. To provide you with the most accurate information, could you please provide us with the tracking number or order number? With this information, we will be able to give you a precise estimate of your shipment's delivery date. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation in this matter.
