User question: I don't know how to see how soon can I expect my parcel

We understand that tracking the estimated arrival time of your parcel is important to you. To provide you with the information you need, we kindly request you to share the {{Tracking Number}} or {{Order Number}} associated with your purchase. Once we have this information, we will be able to give you an accurate estimate of when you can expect your parcel. We appreciate your patience and look forward to assisting you further.
