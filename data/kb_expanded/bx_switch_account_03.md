User question: i do not know how to switch to the {{Account Type}} account

No worries! I'm here to guide you through the process of switching to the {{Account Type}} account:

1. Log in: Start by logging into your account.
2. Account Settings: Once you're logged in, navigate to the '{{Settings}}' section.
3. Switching to Free Account: Look for an option that mentions '{{Account Change}}' or '{{Upgrade Account}}'.
4. Select Free Account: Choose the '{{Account Type}}' option from the available list.
5. Confirmation: Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the switch to the {{Account Type}} account.

If you encounter any difficulties or have additional questions, don't hesitate to let me know. I'm here to help you throughout the process and ensure a smooth transition. Enjoy the benefits of the {{Account Type}} account!"
