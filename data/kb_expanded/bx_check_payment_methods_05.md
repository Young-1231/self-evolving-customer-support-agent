User question: I have got to list the allowed payment modalities, help me

Thank you for reaching out to us for assistance in listing our accepted payment methods. We understand the importance of being able to provide our customers with various options to suit their needs. Here is a comprehensive list of our available payment modalities:

- Credit/Debit Card: We accept major cards such as Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
- PayPal: A secure and widely used online payment method.
- Bank Transfer: Direct payments can be made from your bank account.
- Apple Pay: Quick and convenient payments for Apple users.
- Google Wallet: Google's payment system for fast and easy checkouts.

We hope this list helps you in understanding our accepted payment modalities. Should you have any further questions or require any additional information, please feel free to let us know. We are here to assist you every step of the way.
