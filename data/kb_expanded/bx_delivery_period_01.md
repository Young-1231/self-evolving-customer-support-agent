User question: need help seeing when my packageis going to arrive

We understand your excitement to track the progress of your package and anticipate its arrival. To provide you with the most accurate information, could you please provide us with the {{Tracking Number}} or {{Order Number}}? With that information, we will be able to give you an estimated delivery date. We appreciate your cooperation in helping us serve you better.
