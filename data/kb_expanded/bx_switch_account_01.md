User question: switch user

I'll take care of it! I'm here to guide you through the process of switching users in a seamless manner:

1. Identify the User Icon: Look for the user icon on the top right corner of your screen. It usually appears as a silhouette or a profile picture.
2. Click on the User Icon: Once you've located the user icon, click on it to open the user menu.
3. Select the Switch User Option: In the user menu, you'll find an option labeled "Switch User" or "Log Out". Click on this option to proceed.
4. Choose the Desired User: A list of available user profiles will be displayed. Choose the user you wish to switch to by clicking on their name or profile picture.
5. Enjoy the Switch: Once you've made your selection, the page will refresh, and you'll be switched to the chosen user profile.

If you encounter any difficulties or have further questions, please don't hesitate to ask. Your satisfaction is important to us, and I'm here to ensure a smooth transition between users.
