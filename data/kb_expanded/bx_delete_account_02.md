User question: I don't know how I can remove my standard account

Thank you for contacting us with your question about removing your standard account. I understand that you may be unsure about the process, and I'm here to help you navigate through it. To remove your standard account, you can follow these steps: 
1. Log in to your account using your credentials.
2. Navigate to the account settings section.
3. Look for the option to deactivate or delete your account.
4. Follow the on-screen prompts or instructions to complete the removal process.
If you encounter any difficulties or have further questions along the way, please don't hesitate to ask. We're here to support you throughout this process and ensure a smooth account removal experience.
